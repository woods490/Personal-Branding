POINTER - New Students Group's Companion (2020)

POINTER stands for Proses Orientasi dan Pengembangan Karakter. POINTER is a work program of HIMSI's (Himpunan Mahasiswa S1 Sistem Informasi Unair) human resource and development division to help new students adapt in a university environment. Serving as the new students group's companion, my job is to relay information from the event conceptors to the new students, also to get to know them better.
